Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Data Collection

Kegel Morse Hero does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All exercise data, settings, and progress history are stored locally on your device.

No Tracking

Kegel Morse Hero contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking pixels. No internet connection is required to use the app's exercise features.

Optional Backup

Kegel Morse Hero offers optional Google Drive backup and CSV export. These features are user-initiated only. When you use Google Drive backup, your exercise data is transferred to your own Google account. Kegel Morse Hero does not have access to your Google Drive contents.

No Account Required

Kegel Morse Hero does not require account creation. There is no sign-up, no email collection, and no user identification.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Payment information is managed entirely by Apple. Kegel Morse Hero does not process or store payment details.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, visit the full privacy policy at benderlidze.github.io/KegelMorseHeroDocs/privacy.
