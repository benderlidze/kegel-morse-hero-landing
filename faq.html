Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Kegel Morse Hero and pelvic floor training.

What is Kegel Morse Hero?

Kegel Morse Hero is an iOS app that combines pelvic floor (Kegel) exercises with Morse code rhythmic patterns. It uses visual ring animations and haptic feedback to guide squeeze-and-relax timing.

Who are pelvic floor exercises for?

Pelvic floor exercises are recommended for improving bladder control, core stability, and intimate wellness. They are suitable for all adults. Kegel Morse Hero does not replace medical advice — consult a healthcare professional before starting any exercise program.

Is Kegel Morse Hero free?

Kegel Morse Hero is free to download. A subscription unlocks additional features. Subscription periods are monthly, annual, or lifetime. Payment is charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. Manage subscriptions in your Account Settings after purchase.

Does Kegel Morse Hero collect my data?

No. All data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection required for exercise sessions. Kegel Morse Hero has no ads and no tracking. Optional Google Drive backup and CSV export are available.

Do I need an account to use Kegel Morse Hero?

No. Kegel Morse Hero requires no account creation. Download and start training immediately.

What are Morse code tracks?

Each track is a sequence of dots (short squeezes) and dashes (long holds) that spell out a word in Morse code. For example, SOS is three dots, three dashes, three dots. Kegel Morse Hero includes built-in tracks and lets you create custom ones.

Can I use Kegel Morse Hero offline?

Yes. Kegel Morse Hero works entirely offline. All exercise functionality runs locally on your device without an internet connection.

Is this app a medical device?

No. Kegel Morse Hero is provided for educational and informational purposes only. It does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or qualified health provider with any questions about a medical condition.
