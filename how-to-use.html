How To Use Kegel Morse Hero

A step-by-step guide to start pelvic floor training with clear timing.

1

Download and open the app

Kegel Morse Hero is available on the App Store. No account creation is required to begin.

2

Select a Morse code track

Tap the track name (for example, SOS) to choose a built-in track or create your own pattern.

3

Choose your speed

Select 1x, 2x, or 3x speed. Start with 1x if you are new to Kegel exercise sessions.

4

Press play and follow the ring

The ring animation shows when to squeeze and when to relax. Haptic feedback vibrates in sync with the pattern.

5

Complete the session

When the track finishes, the app logs the session automatically. Repeat or choose another track.

6

Review your progress

Use the calendar and history views to see consistency and session details over time.

Tips for effective training

  • Train consistently. Many users aim for 2 to 3 short sessions per day.
  • Use haptic feedback for discreet, eyes-free guidance.
  • Start with slower tracks at 1x speed and increase gradually.
  • Consult a healthcare professional if you are unsure where to start.
